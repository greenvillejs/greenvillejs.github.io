Welcome to GreenvilleJS!

We are Greenville's Javascript group. We meet for about 2 hours once a month in the Next Innovation Center; this includes a break. You can sign up for 10/20/30 minute slots to talk about anything you want. Or you can just come for the free food and beer.

The only rule is there are no rules...except that JavaScript is the dominant topic, and you should probably wear pants. Frameworks, libraries, plugins, MVC, unit testing, and new fangled concepts are all encouraged.
