NEXT MEETUP IS IN FEBRUARY

We have some things in the works for 2015...

If you were at the November meetup, we discussed some ideas on what the future will hold for GreenvilleJS.

function whatWeDo ( ) {

We are Greenville's Javascript group. We meet once a month in the Next Innovation Center. Don't worry, we take breaks and socialize. You can sign up for a slot to talk about anything you want or you can just come for the free food and beer.

The only rule is there are no rules...except that JavaScript is the dominant topic, and you should probably wear pants. Frameworks, libraries, plugins, MVC, unit testing, and new fangled concepts are all encouraged.


Meetings are held on the first Tuesday of every month.

The next meeting is November 4th. Food at 6pm. Talks at 6:30pm
